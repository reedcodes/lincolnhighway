Adventure awaits this fall along the Lincoln Highway

My dream to drive the entirety of the Lincoln Highway is about to become reality! This lofty ambition started 25 years ago, and come October 2022, the journey begins. This site will document the trip, including stories, photos, and reviews.

The Lincoln Highway

The first coast-to-coast road in the United States, the Lincoln Highway has also been called "The Main Street Across America". Originally dedicated in 1913, the current route spans 3,389 miles, at the Legion of Honor in San Franciso on the west, and at Times Square in New York City on the east.
