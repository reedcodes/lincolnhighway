Tasty trail mix recipe

Tags
food stuff
recipes

I mentioned in my last post that trail mix is a classic road trip food. I wanted to make my own, since many store-bought ones include peanuts or milk chocolate. This is a rough recipe that is easily adapted to your favorites! The main things to include are nuts, dried fruit, something crunchy, and something sweet.

Reed's tasty trail mix recipe

  • 2 cups roasted salted cashews
  • 1 cup cinnamon cruch cereal
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup dried blueberries
  • 1 cup sesame sticks
  • 1/2 cup roasted sunflower seeds
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Measure out the ingredients and throw into a large container or storage bag. Mix, mix, mix until everything is good and scattered. Eat!
